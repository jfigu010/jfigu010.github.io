Graduated!

Graduated and it feels awesome!

07/20/2016
So today turns a month after my graduation. I have graduated with a degree (B.S.) in Chemical Engineering from the Universtiy of California, Riverside. I am so proud of myself that all those endless study worrisome nights have paid off. I am uncertain of what the future holds for me, and there is no doubt I have all kinds of feelings inside me (Happiness, Stress, to name some..) but I know that right now I am passing by a phase thats its fundamental for not only my professional career but my personal life.
Having projects in life have always been a priority for me, wether that would be at work, I always tell myself to push for the limits and find something that I have never done/try. I feel is indispensable for a person to experience as much as they can, since new experiences bring teachings and therefore knowledge.
I am really thankful for all the people that were there to give me a helping hand when I needed. That goes to my family, close friends, mentors, to SHPE, and everyone that was riding with me on this crazy journey. This also goes specifically to my study buddies (Jerry, Sabrina) who were always there to confuse each other more and somehow try to learn everything for our classes.
