About Me

I am a fourth year Chemical engineering student at the University of California, Riverside. My field of study is in Chemistry.
I am interested on finding an internship and job in one of the following industries: Manufacturing, Design and Construction, petrochemicals, and food processing. The idea of creating a website started when I wanted to learn more about coding!
I thought it would be great to show and highlight my engineering and leadership skills.
Here I will be showing my professional and academic work, community outreach involvement and personal hobbies/interest. Hello everyone! Here are some updates of my fourth year. I had the opportunity to lead the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers chapter at UCR.
It was a great experience to be part of such organization and to have worked with my executive board. Together we accomplishied many of the goals that we had set for the year.
I will be posting some pictures that will highlight events and conferences that I was part during the year.

Also I had my Senior Design project. Me as well as four other colleagues worked on upgrading a waste water treatment facility which is located in the Autonomous University of Baja California (UABC) in Tijuana, Mexico.
The need for the university to upgrade was to use the effluent treated water for irrigation. Currently the effluent does not meet the mexican daily standards for nitrate concentration.
It was a great project in which I had the opportunity to attend and visit the campus as well as collaborate with the mexican counterparts in the project.

The current design of the project consisted on a pre-treatment screen, a biological reactor, a clarifier and a storage building.
The tertiary design that we proposed to be the best option consisted on adding sand filters (2) before and after a constructed wetland that will help reduce the nitrates and phosphates concentration for the effluent of the water
as well as a UV Disinfection system that will complete the disinfection to use the water for irrigation purposes.
